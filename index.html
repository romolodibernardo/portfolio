About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer (Ph.D.) and researcher in Generative Design and Biomimetics for aerospace applications at the University of Naples Federico II. My expertise focuses on advanced design methods, fluid dynamics, and Computer-aided Engineering (CAE).

My work bridges the gap between theory and practical application.
